# Eazi Cut

Eazi Cut is a luxury African menswear brand focused on bespoke tailoring and premium ready-to-wear fashion.

Our mission is to help modern gentlemen dress with confidence through exceptional craftsmanship, premium fabrics, and timeless design.

Target Customers:

- Executives
- Professionals
- Grooms
- Wedding guests
- Diaspora clients

Core Services:

- Custom Tailoring
- Ready-to-Wear Fashion
- Wedding Collections
- Corporate Native Wear

Business Goal:

Create the leading online luxury tailoring experience in Nigeria and eventually across Africa.
