Document ID: PRD-007

Category: Product Design

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: Mubeejoy Technologies

Project: Eazi Cut Digital Platform

---

# Purpose

This document defines the emotions customers should experience throughout every interaction with Eazi Cut.

Luxury brands do not simply sell products.

They create memorable emotional experiences.

Every page, animation, image, color, interaction, and piece of copy should reinforce these emotions.

---

# Emotional Design Principles

Customers should never feel:

❌ Confused

❌ Rushed

❌ Distrustful

❌ Overwhelmed

❌ Lost

Instead they should always feel:

✔ Welcome

✔ Confident

✔ Inspired

✔ Valued

✔ Proud

✔ Excited

✔ Reassured

✔ Exclusive

What do we want the customer to click?
