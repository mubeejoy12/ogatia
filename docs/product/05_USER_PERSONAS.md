# User Personas

**Project:** Eazi Cut Digital Platform

**Version:** 1.0

**Owner:** Mubeejoy Technologies

---

# Purpose

This document defines the primary customer personas for the Eazi Cut platform.

Every design decision, feature, marketing campaign, and engineering decision should solve a real customer problem identified here.

Whenever there is uncertainty about a feature, ask:

> "Which persona does this improve?"

---

# Persona 1 — The Executive

## Profile

Name: David

Age: 38

Occupation: Bank Executive

Income: High

Location: Lagos

Relationship Status: Married

Technology Level: High

---

## About

David is a successful professional who values appearance.

He attends business meetings, conferences, dinners, weddings, and networking events.

His clothing reflects his status.

---

## Goals

• Look professional

• Save time

• Buy premium clothing

• Get perfect fitting garments

• Build a wardrobe that lasts

---

## Pain Points

• Busy schedule

• No time for repeated tailor visits

• Inconsistent tailoring quality

• Poor communication from local tailors

---

## Needs

✔ Luxury experience

✔ Fast communication

✔ Easy ordering

✔ Saved measurements

✔ Premium fabrics

✔ Reliable delivery

---

## Features That Matter Most

- Saved measurements

- Order tracking

- Appointment booking (future)

- Premium collections

- Easy checkout

# Customer Priorities

Across all personas, customers consistently value:

1. Trust
2. Quality
3. Professionalism
4. Excellent customer service
5. Easy ordering
6. Clear communication
7. Reliable delivery
8. Premium presentation

---

# Shared Expectations

Every customer expects:

- Fast website
- Mobile-friendly experience
- Secure payments
- High-quality photography
- Transparent pricing
- Accurate order updates
- Professional communication

---

# Design Implications

The interface should:

- Feel premium
- Reduce complexity
- Build trust immediately
- Minimize clicks
- Guide customers naturally
- Highlight craftsmanship

---

# Engineering Implications

Developers should prioritize:

- Performance
- Reliability
- Security
- Mobile responsiveness
- Accessibility
- Scalability

---

# Success Criteria

A successful Eazi Cut experience makes customers feel:

- Confident
- Valued
- Inspired
- In control
- Excited to receive their garments

---

# Guiding Principle

We are not building features.

We are solving problems for real people.

Every screen should answer one question:

> "How does this improve the customer's experience?"
